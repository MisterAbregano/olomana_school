Mr. R Abregano

Born in Honolulu, Hawaiʻi, "Mr. A" loved the ocean until slowly losing his vision. He adapted and became a video game enthusiast, sports fan, and martial arts practitioner. He currently dances hula with his awesome and talented wife, Mrs. Alexis (also known lovingly as Aunty Sweets 💃🏽) for Hālau ʻO Maluakiʻiwailehua under Kumu Kuana Torres Kahele. His all-time favorite thing to do is sing and play Hawaiian music.

It’s about resilience: believing in your cause and living every moment towards success. No matter what the odds are; deny the doubters. There is zero tolerance for bullying in my classroom. Please respect each others space and time.

Mr. Abregano was employed as an Electrician by IBEW 1186. Because of his time in Michigan, he is a lifelong sports fan of the 313 "Detroit Motor City" — a place known for its Blue-Collar GRIT and the pursuit of the all American dream: fast cars, Motown music, MegaTron, and good ol’ Barry. When he says out loud, “Detroit vs. The World,”! That's all about resilience, supporting your squad; no matter the odds. It all begins with the Lions, then the Pistons, the Red Wings, and D's Tigers! Mr. A spends a lot of weekends supporting his fam while experiencing new vibes with friends.
