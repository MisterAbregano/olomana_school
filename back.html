📖 Student Handbook

Ryan Abregano

I am a cultural educator and mentor who draws on my own journey through school and life in Hawaiʻi to guide students toward academic and personal success. Whether helping a learner tackle a tough assignment or coaching a small group project, I bring empathy, patience, and a healthy dose of local ʻāina wisdom. By blending hands-on activities, collaborative tech tools, and real-world examples, my goal is to empower every student to discover their strengths, set meaningful goals, and grow into confident, creative contributors to our community.
